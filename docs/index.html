art by
Danielle Navarro

I’m a computational artist based in Sydney, living on Gadigal land, and making generative art using the R programming language. I post artwork on twitter, instagram and github and in my other life I am an academic at the University of New South Wales. This site is written with R, using the diziet theme for hugodown.

At the moment I don’t sell prints but I intend to do so in the near future. Unless stated otherwise, the art on this site is released under a Creative Commons CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0 licence. If you want to use it for commercial purposes please get in touch with me!

If you like my art and would like to show your support, my PayPal is daniellenavarro77.
